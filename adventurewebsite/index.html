bungee jump

Bungee jumping involves a person jumping from a great height while connected to a large elastic cord. The launching pad is usually erected on a tall structure such as a building or crane, a bridge across a deep ravine.

read more

zip lines

A zip-line is a pulley suspended on a cable, mounted on a slope. It is designed to enable cargo or a person propelled by gravity to travel from the top to the bottom of the inclined cable by holding on to, the freely moving pulley.

read more

Canoeing

Canoeing is an excellent way of exploring natural or cultural landscapes along a suitable waterway. A canoe can navigate very shallow and narrow passages, and brings the environment very close to the traveller.

read more

kayaking

Kayaking involves the use of a kayak, a narrowboat with a double-blade paddle to move on water. The driver is supposed to navigate through the waterways, sitting face-forward, marching ahead with side-to-side paddle strokes.
